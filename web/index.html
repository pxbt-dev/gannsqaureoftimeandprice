🌀 Square of Nine Grid

How to read the Square of Nine: This is W.D. Gann's famous spiral grid, starting from 1 at the center and spiralling outward. Numbers on the cardinal cross (horizontal & vertical through the center) represent strong support/resistance levels. Numbers on the ordinal cross (diagonals) represent secondary support/resistance. In Gann theory, the square root of price is placed on the wheel — prices that align on the same cross as your base price are natural turning points.
